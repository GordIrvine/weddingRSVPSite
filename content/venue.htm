The wedding will be taking place at the beautiful Rufflets Country House Hotel outside St Andrews

There are rooms available at Rufflets but as it is quite expensive we recommend the Premier Inn St Andrews or the Premier Inn Dundee Centre as cheaper alternatives. There are also several other hotels, guest houses and B&B's nearby so you can choose the accommodation that best suits your needs.

Dundee has better transport links from both Edinburgh and Glasgow but St Andrews itself is obviously closer to the venue.

If flying into Scotland we recommend using Edinburgh Airport as your destination. It is the closest airport to St Andrews.

Rufflets Country House Hotel

Set in 10 acres of award winning gardens on the outskirts of St Andrews, the hotel and grounds provide an idyllic backdrop for your stay. It is located to the West of St Andrews Town Centre with the beaches and golf courses in the centre of town all no more than 5 minutes away by car.

Directions:

Driving:

See the Rufflets website for detailed instructions.

Bus:

From Edinburgh Airport take the Jet 747 to Ferrytoll Park and Ride. Then take the X59 to St Andrews Bus Station. Rufflets is then a short taxi ride away.

Premier Inn St Andrews

The Premier Inn St Andrews is a 10 minute drive from Rufflets. The public transport links from Edinburgh and Glasgow are not ideal but there are buses and trains available (details below).

There is no preferential rate but it's fairly cheap when booked in advance.

Directions:

Driving:

Follow A91 towards St Andrews. In St Andrews, follow signs for Kircaldy (A915) - this will take you right at a roundabout. Continue straight over 2 more roundabouts whichwill lead to Largo road. The hotel is located on the right opposite the VW car showroom.

Bus:

From Edinburgh Airport take the Jet 747 to Ferrytoll Park and Ride. Then take the X59 to St Andrews Bus Station. The Premier Inn St Andrews is a 15 minute walk (or 3 minute taxi ride) away.

Premier Inn Dundee Center

The Premier Inn Dundee Center is situated on the edge of the River Tay 25 minute taxi ride from Rufflets. It's situated close to Dundee train station and has the benefit of better transport links from Edinburgh and Glasgow than St Andrews.

There is no preferential rate but it's fairly cheap when booked in advance.

Directions:

Driving:

From Edinburgh/Glasgow: Follow M90 towards Perth then take A90 towards Dundee. Continue to follow signs for the City Centre then Discovery Point. The hotel is the next left after Discovery Point, opposite the railway station.

Train:

From Edinburgh Airport take the Airlink 100 Bus (Stop D) to Waverly Bridge. Waverly Station is then accross the road and a train to Dundee can be taken. The Premier Inn is located accross the street from the station.

For full timetables and to book tickets see Scotrail.co.uk
