Premier Inn | Dundee Centre

The wedding will be taking place at the beautiful Rufflets Country House Hotel outside St Andrews

There are rooms available at Rufflets but as it is quite expensive we reccommend the Premier Inn Dundee Centre as a cheaper alternative. It's 25 minutes from the venue and, depending on numbers, we would potentially like have a coach available to shuttle everyone to the venue and back.

If flying into Scotland we reccomend using Edinburgh Airport as your destination. It is the closest airport to St Andrews.

Rufflets Country House Hotel

Set in 10 acres of award winning gardens on the outskirts of St Andrews, the hotel and grounds provide an idyllic backdrop for your stay. It is located to the West of St Andrews Town Centre with the beaches and golf courses in the centre of town all no more than 5 minutes away by car.

As part of the wedding booking we have 8 rooms available at a reduced rate that are available to any of our guests who wish to stay at the venue. These are available on a first come first served basis.

Premier Inn Dundee Center

The Premier Inn Dundee Center is situated on the edge of the River Tay 25 minutes from Rufflets. It's situated close to Dundee train station and has the benefit of better transport links from Edinburgh and Glasgow than St Andrews.

There is no preferential rate but it's fairly cheap when booked in advance.

Directions:

Driving:

From Edinburgh/Glasgow: Follow M90 towards Perth then take A90 towards Dundee. Continue to follow signs for the City Centre then Discovery Point. The hotel is the next left after Discovery Point, opposite the railway station.

Bus & Train:

From Edinburgh Airport take the Airlink 100 (Stop D) to Waverly Bridge. Waverly Station is then accross the road and a train to Dundee can be taken. The Premier Inn is located accross the street from the station.
