Cornhill Macmillan Centre

In late 2016 my mum, Marian, passed away after a year long struggle against cancer. It was a very difficult and heartbreaking time for us and I'm eternally grateful to everyone at the Cornhill centre who cared for her in her final weeks before she died.

We miss her a lot and I know she would appreciate everything the centre did for both her and us in those final weeks and would want us to give something back.

The centre provides day, community and in-patient palliative care services. The in-patient unit is the first of its kind in Perth and Kinross and means that patients and their families no longer have to travel for specialist in-patient palliative care.
